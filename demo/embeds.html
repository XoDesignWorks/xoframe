YouTube facade

The real YouTube embed costs ~1 MB of JS before the user presses play. This facade is just a poster and a button; the iframe is injected on click. Hovering preconnects to YouTube so playback starts faster.

Intent strategy

This image has data-xo-strategy="intent" — it loads on the first hover/focus/touch, not on scroll. Great for tab panels and dropdown previews.
